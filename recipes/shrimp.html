Quick Shrimp

Description:

This is how I prepare shrimp. You can eat these chilled, with cocktail sauce or add them to your favorite recipe. I love to add these shrimp to coctel de camaron (Mexican shrimp cocktail).

Ingredients:

  • 2 Lbs Shrimp (thawed)
  • Old Bay Seasoning (to taste)
  • 2-3 Fresh Lemons

Steps:

  1. Add Old bay to water with 1-2 sliced lemons.
  2. Place shrimp in water and let boil for 4 minutes Or until shrimp break apart and are fully cooked
  3. Gar nish with fresh lemon and enjoy!
